Our Story

Our Website began in 2019. After years in the web hosting industry, we realized that it was near impossible for the average Jane or Joe to be safe on any website. Traditional antivirus services were simply too complicated,and expensive.

We created the Light Web with the perspective of everyday people in mind. We wanted to offer a platform that would require no hidden fees or back doors. We keep it simple, so users can focus on the work.

Today, we're proud to empower individuals and small business owners around the world to be safe on the web.
